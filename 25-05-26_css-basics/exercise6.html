Easy tilapia recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Half cup Lemon Juice
  • Half cup Kosher Salt
  • 4 Tilapia Fish
  • Quarter spoon Sweet Paprika
  • Quarter spoon Turmeric
  • 4 spoons Olive Oil
  • 3 spoons Water
  • Half spoon Ground Pepper

The proccess:

  1. Marinating the fish: Soak the fish for about half an hour in lemon juice and a little coarse salt.
  2. After marinating, rinse the tilapia fillets very well and arrange them on baking paper in an oven tray.
  3. Seasoning and brushing: In a small bowl, mix the oil together with all the spices.
  4. Using a silicone brush, brush the spice paste onto the tilapia fillets; it is important to brush the tilapia on all sides.
  5. Baking the fish in the oven: Bake in an oven preheated to 200°C for 12–14 minutes (depending on the size of the fish), until the tilapia gets a nice color.
